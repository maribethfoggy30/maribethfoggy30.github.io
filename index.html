Quantum error mitigation Completed study

Bayesian-Informed Zero-Noise Extrapolation

A framework combining zero-noise extrapolation with simulation-derived Bayesian priors to improve the stability and accuracy of expectation-value estimates from noisy quantum hardware.

  • Qiskit
  • IBM Quantum
  • Bayesian inference
  • Noise folding
Initial result MAE reduced by approximately 87%

In a reported three-qubit experiment, the Bayesian estimate was substantially closer to the ideal value than the unconstrained extrapolation.

Quantum error mitigation Ongoing

Quantum-Derived Priors for Scalable Error Mitigation

Extending Bayesian-informed error mitigation beyond classically simulable systems using quantum shadows, calibration-derived information, and limited process characterization of high-impact circuit regions.

  • Classical shadows
  • Process matrices
  • Calibration data
  • VQE
Quantum sensing Research proposal

Hadron Irradiation of NV-Center Diamonds

A proposed study of how GeV-scale hadron irradiation changes the spin coherence and sensing performance of nitrogen-vacancy centers in diamond, with relevance to radiation-rich environments.

  • NV centers
  • Spin coherence
  • Radiation damage
  • Quantum sensing
